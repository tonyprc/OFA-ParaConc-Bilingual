<seg id="1">二〇一六年新年贺词</seg>
<seg id="1">2016 New Year Address</seg>
<seg id="2">中国国家主席 习近平</seg>
<seg id="2">By Xi Jinping President of People’s Republic of China</seg>
<seg id="3">2015年12月31日，北京</seg>
<seg id="3">Beijing, December 31, 2015</seg>
<seg id="4">同志们，朋友们，女士们，先生们：</seg>
<seg id="4">Comrades, friends, ladies and gentlemen,</seg>
<seg id="5">再过几个小时，新年的钟声就要敲响了。</seg>
<seg id="5">In a few hours, the New Year bell will be ringing.</seg>
<seg id="6">我们即将告别2015年，迎来2016年的第一缕阳光。</seg>
<seg id="6">We will say goodbye to the year 2015 and welcome the first ray of sunshine of the year 2016.</seg>
<seg id="7">在这辞旧迎新的时刻，我向全国各族人民，向香港特别行政区同胞和澳门特别行政区同胞，向台湾同胞和海外侨胞，向世界各国和各地区的朋友们，致以新年的祝福！</seg>
<seg id="7">At this turn of the year, I wish to extend my New Year greetings to the people of all ethnic groups in China, to our compatriots in the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region and the Macao Special Administrative Region, to our compatriots in Taiwan and overseas Chinese, as well as friends in other countries and regions in the world.</seg>
<seg id="8">有付出，就会有收获。</seg>
<seg id="8">As long as we pay, there will be gains.</seg>
<seg id="9">2015年，中国人民付出了很多，也收获了很多。</seg>
<seg id="9">In 2015, the great efforts of the Chinese people have paid off.</seg>
<seg id="10">我国经济增长继续居于世界前列，改革全面发力，司法体制改革继续深化，“三严三实”专题教育推动了政治生态改善，反腐败斗争深入进行。</seg>
<seg id="10">China's economic growth continues to lead in the world. Reform has been pushed forward comprehensively. Reform in the judicial system has been further deepened. The special educational campaign of "Three Stricts and Three Earnests" has promoted the improvement of the political eco-system. The fight against corruption has been carried out deeply.</seg>
<seg id="11">经过全国各族人民共同努力，“十二五”规划圆满收官，广大人民群众有了更多获得感。</seg>
<seg id="11">Through the joint efforts of the people of all ethnic groups across the country, we see a successful conclusion of the "12th Five-Year Plan". The general public has enjoyed the increasing "sense of gain".</seg>
<seg id="12">这一年，我们隆重纪念了中国人民抗日战争暨世界反法西斯战争胜利70周年，举行了盛大阅兵，昭示了正义必胜、和平必胜、人民必胜的真理。</seg>
<seg id="12">During this past year, we solemnly commemorated the 70th anniversary of the Victory of the Chinese People's War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression and the World Anti-Fascist War. We held a grand military parade, making the truth clear to all that justice will prevail, peace will prevail, and the people will prevail.</seg>
<seg id="13">我们全面实施改革强军战略，宣布裁军30万。</seg>
<seg id="13">We will comprehensively implement the reform strategy to strengthen China's military, and we announced China will cut the number of its troops by 300, 000.</seg>
<seg id="14">我和马英九先生在新加坡会面，实现了跨越66年时空的握手，表明两岸关系和平发展是两岸同胞的共同心愿。</seg>
<seg id="14">Mr. Ma Ying-jeou and I met in Singapore, with a handshake that transcended 66 years of time and space. This shows the peaceful development of cross-Strait relations is the common wish of the people on both sides of the Taiwan Strait.</seg>
<seg id="15">这一年，北京获得第24届冬奥会举办权，人民币纳入国际货币基金组织特别提款权货币篮子，我国自主研制的C919大型客机总装下线，中国超级计算机破世界纪录蝉联“六连冠”，我国科学家研制的暗物质探测卫星发射升空，屠呦呦成为我国首位获得诺贝尔奖的科学家……</seg>
<seg id="15">During the past year, Beijing won the bid to host the 24thWinter Olympic Games. The renminbi has been included into the Special Drawing Rights basket of the International Monetary Fund. China's domestically-produced C919 large passenger aircraft rolled off the production line. China's super computer broke the world record for a sixth consecutive year. A satellite developed by Chinese scientists detect "dark matter" was launched. Tu Youyou became China's first scientist to win a Nobel Prize.</seg>
<seg id="16">这说明，只要坚持，梦想总是可以实现的。</seg>
<seg id="16">These show that as long as we persevere, dreams can always be realized.</seg>
<seg id="17">这一年，我们有欣喜，也有悲伤。</seg>
<seg id="17">During the past year, we had happiness, as well as sadness.</seg>
<seg id="18">“东方之星”号客轮翻沉、天津港特别重大火灾爆炸、深圳滑坡等事故造成不少同胞失去了生命，还有我们的同胞被恐怖分子残忍杀害，令人深感痛心。</seg>
<seg id="18">The capsizing of the "Eastern Star" ferry, the major fire and explosions at Tianjin Port, the Shenzhen landslide and other accidents have taken the lives of many of our countrymen. And some countrymen were brutally killed by terrorists. All these are deeply heartbreaking.</seg>
<seg id="19">我们怀念他们，愿逝者安息、生者安康！</seg>
<seg id="19">We will remember them. We wish all the deceased to rest in peace and the living safe and healthy.</seg>
<seg id="20">群众的生活中还有一些困难和烦恼。</seg>
<seg id="20">Some difficulties and troubles still remain in people's daily life.</seg>
<seg id="21">党和政府一定会继续努力，切实保障人民生命财产安全、保障人民生活改善、保障人民身体健康。</seg>
<seg id="21">The Communist Party of China and the government will surely continue efforts to effectively guarantee the safety of people's lives and property, guarantee the improvement f people's livelihood, and guarantee people's health.</seg>
<seg id="22">同志们，朋友们，女士们，先生们，</seg>
<seg id="22">Comrades, friends, ladies and gentlemen,</seg>
<seg id="23">2016年是我国进入全面建成小康社会决胜阶段的开局之年。</seg>
<seg id="23">The year 2016 is the first year when China enters the crucial period to build a moderately well-off society in all all-around way.</seg>
<seg id="24">中共十八届五中全会明确了未来5年我国发展的方向。</seg>
<seg id="24">The 5th Plenary Session of the 18th CPC Central Committee has made clear China's development direction for the next five years.</seg>
<seg id="25">前景令人鼓舞、催人奋进，但幸福不会从天降。</seg>
<seg id="25">The future is encouraging and inspiring. But happiness does not fall from the sky.</seg>
<seg id="26">我们要树立必胜信念、继续埋头苦干，贯彻创新、协调、绿色、开放、共享的发展理念，着力推进结构性改革，着力推进改革开放，着力促进社会公平正义，着力营造政治上的绿水青山，为全面建成小康社会决胜阶段开好局、起好步。</seg>
<seg id="26">We shall establish a spirit to prevail, continue to immerse ourselves in hard work, implement the development concepts of innovation, coordination, green, openness and sharing. We shall put forth the efforts in promoting structural reform, and reform and opening up, promoting social fairness and justice, as well as creating a green political eco-system. We shall get off to a good start as we advance in the crucial period for China to build a moderately well-off society in all all-around way.</seg>
<seg id="27">全面建成小康社会，13亿人要携手前进。</seg>
<seg id="27">To build a moderately well-off society in all all-around way, our 1.3 billion people should joint hands and move forward together.</seg>
<seg id="28">让几千万农村贫困人口生活好起来，是我心中的牵挂。</seg>
<seg id="28">A better life for tens of millions of poor people in rural regions is dear to my heart.</seg>
<seg id="29">我们吹响了打赢扶贫攻坚战的号角，全党全国要勠力同心，着力补齐这块短板，确保农村所有贫困人口如期摆脱贫困。</seg>
<seg id="29">We have sounded the trumpet to win the battle of poverty alleviation. All Party members and the entire nation should pull together and work hard as a team, put forth efforts to lengthen this short stave. We should make sure that the entire poor population in rural regions can rise out of poverty on schedule.</seg>
<seg id="30">对所有困难群众，我们都要关爱，让他们从内心感受到温暖。</seg>
<seg id="30">For all the people in difficulties, we should care for them and let them feel the warmth from deep in their hearts.</seg>
<seg id="31">同志们，朋友们，女士们，先生们，</seg>
<seg id="31">Comrades, friends, ladies and gentlemen,</seg>
<seg id="32">我们只有一个地球，这是各国人民共同的家园。</seg>
<seg id="32">We have only one earth, which is the common home of people of all countries.</seg>
<seg id="33">这一年，我国领导人参加了不少国际会议，开展了不少外交活动，推动“一带一路”建设取得实质性进展，参与了联合国2030年可持续发展议程、应对全球气候变化等国际事务。</seg>
<seg id="33">During the past year, Chinese leaders have participated in many international conferences and conducted many diplomatic activities. We have achieved solid progress in promoting the development of the “Belt and Road” initiative. We engaged in many international affairs including the United Nations 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development and issues on dealing with global climate change.</seg>
<seg id="34">世界那么大，问题那么多，国际社会期待听到中国声音、看到中国方案，中国不能缺席。</seg>
<seg id="34">The world is so big, and the problems are so many. The international community expects to hear China's voice and see China's plans. China cannot be absent.</seg>
<seg id="35">面对身陷苦难和战火的人们，我们要有悲悯和同情，更要有责任和行动。</seg>
<seg id="35">Seeing the people trapped deep in suffering and war, we should have compassion and sympathy, but also take responsibilities and action.</seg>
<seg id="36">中国将永远向世界敞开怀抱，也将尽己所能向面临困境的人们伸出援手，让我们的“朋友圈”越来越大。</seg>
<seg id="36">China will always open its arms to the world and will make all efforts possible to extend our helping hand to the people facing difficulties. Let our "circle of friends" grow bigger and bigger.</seg>
<seg id="37">我衷心希望，国际社会共同努力，多一份平和，多一份合作，变对抗为合作，化干戈为玉帛，共同构建各国人民共有共享的人类命运共同体。</seg>
<seg id="37">I sincerely hope that the international community can work together. With more peacefulness and more cooperation let's turn confrontation into cooperation, and turn swords into ploughshares. Let's work together to build a community of common destiny of all mankind, which is shared by all people in every country.</seg>
<seg id="38">谢谢大家。</seg>
<seg id="38">Thank you all.</seg>
