<seg id="1">二〇一四年新年贺词</seg>
<seg id="1">2014 New Year Address</seg>
<seg id="2">中国国家主席 习近平</seg>
<seg id="2">By Xi Jinping President of People’s Republic of China</seg>
<seg id="3">2013年12月31日，北京</seg>
<seg id="3">Beijing, December 31, 2013</seg>
<seg id="4">女士们、先生们、同志们、朋友们：</seg>
<seg id="4">Ladies and gentlemen, comrades and friends:</seg>
<seg id="5">我们即将迎来充满希望的2014年。</seg>
<seg id="5">We'll soon usher in the year 2014 with great hopes.</seg>
<seg id="6">一元复始，万象更新。</seg>
<seg id="6">As the new year begins, let us also start anew.</seg>
<seg id="7">在这里，我向全国各族人民，向香港特别行政区同胞和澳门特别行政区同胞，向台湾同胞和海外侨胞，向世界各国和各地区的朋友们，致以新年的祝福！</seg>
<seg id="7">I'm delighted to extend New Year wishes to Chinese people of all ethnic groups, to compatriots in the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, the Macao Special Administrative Region and Taiwan, to overseas Chinese and to friends all over the world!</seg>
<seg id="8">祝福老人们健康！祝福孩子们快乐！祝福每个家庭幸福安康！</seg>
<seg id="8">I wish senior people and children, and every family happiness, peace and health in the New Year.</seg>
<seg id="9">在这辞旧迎新之际，无数工人、农民、知识分子、干部仍然坚守在工作岗位，不少同胞依然奔波在世界各地为祖国辛勤工作，许多人民解放军和武警官兵、公安干警正在履行光荣使命。</seg>
<seg id="9">At a time to ring out the old year and ring in the new, countless workers, farmers, intellectuals and cadres still remain at their posts working hard. Many of our compatriots are on the go, working in various regions of the world for the motherland. Many members of the People's Liberation Army, armed police and public security are performing their glorious duties.</seg>
<seg id="10">他们有的远离祖国、远离亲人，有的不能同家人团聚。</seg>
<seg id="10">Some of them are far away from the motherland, and their close relatives and some cannot have a reunion with family members.</seg>
<seg id="11">我代表祖国和人民，向他们致以诚挚的问候，祝他们平安顺利！</seg>
<seg id="11">On behalf of the motherland and people, I'd like to offer my sincere greetings to them and wish them a peaceful and smooth new year.</seg>
<seg id="12">2013年，对我们国家和人民来说是很不平凡的一年。</seg>
<seg id="12">The year 2013 has been an extraordinary year for our nation and people.</seg>
<seg id="13">我们共同战胜了各种困难和挑战，取得了新的显著成就。</seg>
<seg id="13">We have worked together in overcoming a variety of obstacles and challenges, and made new outstanding achievements.</seg>
<seg id="14">成绩来之不易，凝聚了大家的心血和汗水。</seg>
<seg id="14">The achievements have not come easily as they are the result of blood and sweat from all.</seg>
<seg id="15">我向大家表示衷心的感谢！</seg>
<seg id="15">I'd like to convey my heartfelt gratitude to you all.</seg>
<seg id="16">2013年，我们对全面深化改革做出总体部署，共同描绘了未来发展的宏伟蓝图。</seg>
<seg id="16">In 2013, we made an overall plan on comprehensively deepening reform and laid out a grand blueprint together for future development.</seg>
<seg id="17">2014年，我们将在改革的道路上迈出新的步伐。</seg>
<seg id="17">In 2014, we are expected to make new strides on the road of reform.</seg>
<seg id="18">我们推进改革的根本目的，是要让国家变得更加富强、让社会变得更加公平正义、让人民生活得更加美好。</seg>
<seg id="18">We have advanced reforms with the fundamental purpose of making our nation stronger and more prosperous. We also aim to achieve a higher degree of impartiality and justice in society so that people can live a better life.</seg>
<seg id="19">改革是需要我们共同为之奋斗的伟大事业，需要付出艰辛的努力。</seg>
<seg id="19">Reform is a great cause that calls for common commitments and strenuous efforts from us all.</seg>
<seg id="20">一分耕耘，一分收获。</seg>
<seg id="20">We must sow before we can reap.</seg>
<seg id="21">在改革开放的伟大实践中，我们已经创造了无数辉煌。</seg>
<seg id="21">In the great practice of reform and opening up, we have already made countless spectacular achievements.</seg>
<seg id="22">我坚信，中国人民必将创造出新的辉煌。</seg>
<seg id="22">I firmly believe that the Chinese people will create new spectacles.</seg>
<seg id="23">宇宙浩瀚，星汉灿烂。</seg>
<seg id="23">There exists an immense and vast universe with bright stars.</seg>
<seg id="24">70多亿人共同生活在我们这个星球上，应该守望相助、同舟共济、共同发展。</seg>
<seg id="24">More than 7 billion people inhabit the planet Earth. We in the same boat should keep watch and help each other to achieve common development.</seg>
<seg id="25">中国人民追寻实现中华民族伟大复兴的中国梦，也祝愿各国人民能够实现自己的梦想。</seg>
<seg id="25">We Chinese people seek to realize the Chinese dream, a great revitalization of the Chinese nation, and also wish that the dreams of people of all countries will come true.</seg>
<seg id="26">我真诚希望，世界各国人民在实现各自梦想的过程中相互理解、相互帮助，努力把我们赖以生存的地球建设成为共同的美好家园。</seg>
<seg id="26">I sincerely hope that people of various countries in the world will understand and help each other in the course of realizing their dreams and build the Earth that we rely on for survival into a common beautiful homeland.</seg>
<seg id="27">生活总是充满希望的，成功总是属于积极进取、不懈追求的人们。</seg>
<seg id="27">Life is always full of hopes. Success always belongs to those who keep forging ahead unswervingly.</seg>
<seg id="28">我们在前进的道路上，还会遇到各种风险和挑战。</seg>
<seg id="28">On our way forward, we will encounter various risks and challenges.</seg>
<seg id="29">让老百姓过上更加幸福的生活，还有大量工作要做。</seg>
<seg id="29">We still need to complete a lot of tasks so that common people can live a happier life.</seg>
<seg id="30">我们要谦虚谨慎、艰苦奋斗，共同谱写伟大祖国发展的时代新篇章。</seg>
<seg id="30">We need to be modest and prudent and work hard together in writing a new chapter for the development of our great nation.</seg>
<seg id="31">谢谢大家。</seg>
<seg id="31">Thank you all.</seg>
